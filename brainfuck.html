Stumbling upon this esoteric language called brainfuck on youtube, I found it very interesting, so I learned it to understand it's process. The core concepts of this language is easy. you are working on a stack, and you are given only a few things to work with
+=Add one
-=Subtract one
>=Move to the left one
<=Move to the right one
[]=Loop until the leftmost cell is zero.
.=Display block of manipulated cells
,=Accept user input, store into cell
This translator works with all these elements to output your english sentence into a brainfuck sentence.
